About Winter Sports

Winter sports are competitive sports or recreational activities which are played on snow or ice.

This website explores three popular disciplines:

  • Ice Hockey - A fast-paced team sport.
  • Figure Skating - An artistic performance on ice.
  • Skiing - A thrilling race down the slopes.

Where are the 2026 Winter Olympics?

The 2026 Winter Games are being held in Milan and Cortina d'Ampezzo, Italy.

The Opening Ceremony was held at the San Siro Stadium in Milan.
